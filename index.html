About us

Learning by making.
Making together.

Hack Club CV started in 2026 (by a sophomore!) when a group of students at CVHS decided they wanted a space to build projects outside of class.

We're part of the global Hack Club network, but our community is uniquely ours. We run workshops, host hackathons, and spend a lot of time debugging code together (and occasionally debugging each other's life choices).

Our philosophy is simple: you learn best when you're building things you care about, surrounded by people who support you. No grades, no pressure‚Äîjust genuine curiosity and the joy of making things work.
